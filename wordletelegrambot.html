Description
Wordle Telegram Bot is a bot I wrote so that I could play wordle with my friends on the popular messaging application, Telegram. By adding the bot to your group chat, you can start a game by setting a word and have your friends guess away! You can add the bot to your group chat by searching for its handle "@lingowordle_bot".
Tools used
Python, Telegram Bot API
